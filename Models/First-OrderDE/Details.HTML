Details

The differential equation introduced in Overview is

(1) dY/dX = - K * Y

with Y0 being the initial value of Y.

Equation (1) can also be expressed as an integral equation.

(4) Y = (dY/dX) dX

in conjunction with

(1) dY/dX = - K * Y

The exact solution to Equations (4) and (1) is

(5) Exact_Y = Y0 * exp (-K * X)

The error in the calculated solution is

(6) Error = Exact_Y - Calculated_Y

As noted, this is one of the few differential equations that has an exact solution. For all others, numerical solution is required.
