Overview

Values represent plasma at 37C. Units are Eq / L.

Water dissociation

[H+] * [OH-] = K'w
K'w = 4.4e-14

Weak acid dissociation

[H+] * [A-] = Ka * [HA]
Ka = 2.0e-7

Weak acid conservation

[HA] + [A-] = [ATot]

Bicarbonate formation

[H+] * [HCO3-] = Kc * pCO2
Kc = 2.58e-11

Carbonate formation

[H+] * [CO3--] = K3 * [HCO3-]
K3 = 6.0e-11

Electrical neutrality

[SID] + [H+] - [HCO3-] - [A-] - [CO3--] - [OH-] = 0

The rate constants and conditions of electric neutrality lead to a big implicit algebraic equation.
