Details

X is the model's independent variable. It represents time in days.

H is the healthy group head count (Persons).

dH/dX is the healthy group's rate of change (Persons/Day).

C is the contagious group head count (Persons).

dC/dX is the contagious group's rate of change (Persons/Day).

R is the recovered group head count (Persons).

dR/dX is the recovered group's rate of change (Persons/Day).

C(0) is the inital contagion head count.

Virulence is the virulence of the epidemic. Units are 1/Person-Day.

ContagionRate is the epidemic's contagion rate (Persons/Day).

RecoverySpeed is the epidemic's recovery speed. Units are 1/Day.

RecoveryRate is the epidemic's recovery rate (Persons/Day).
