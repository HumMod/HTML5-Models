Overview

Substrate (S) and enzyme (E) combine to form the enzyme/substrate complex (ES). The complex then dissociates to yield enzyme (E) plus product (P).

There are 4 differential equations, representing

E - Enzyme
S - Substrate
ES - Enzyme/Substrate Complex
P - Product

There are 4 rate constants controlling

k1 : E + S --> ES
k2 : ES --> E + S
k3 : ES --> E + P
k4 : E + P --> ES
