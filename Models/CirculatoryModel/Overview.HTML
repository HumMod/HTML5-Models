Overview

This is a basic model of circulatory system function. The model includes

  • Both peripheral and cardiac contributions to cardiac output.
  • The peripheral contribution involves blood volume, mean circulatory filling pressure, resistance to venous return and right atrial pressure.
  • The cardiac contribution involves heart strength and right atrial pressure.
  • Basic fluid balance involving salt and water intake and renal salt and water excretion.
  • Peripheral autoregulation of blood flow.
  • Arterial pressure is a function of peripheral vascular resistance and cardiac output.
